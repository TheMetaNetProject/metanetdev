Stop the madness, enact sensible gun laws

April 12, 2014

How much longer do we have to tolerate daily, multiple, senseless gun deaths? In 2012, there were 32,000 such deaths. In each of the last couple of years, there have been more civilian gun-related deaths than there have been soldiers killed in the many wars we have fought.

We must put a stop to the convoluted thinking of the NRA. It is imperative that we pass some gun-control laws. It is time for our legislators to stand up to the NRA and the gun manufacturers lobby.

Please write or call your representatives and tell them to pass gun control measures to stop this mayhem.

Seymour Landau, Plantation
